## Test Objective
Verify kebab menu appears at 100% course completion and "Sync progress now" option is enabled and functional.

Start an Appium session and launch the Sunbird Ed app on an Android Emulator.

Click on the Profile at the bottom and click

Locate and click the kebab menu (3 dots) in the course progress section.

Verify "Sync progress now" option is enabled (not grayed out).

Verify success message appears (toast notification or confirmation).
